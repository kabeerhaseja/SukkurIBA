About Us

Mr. Nisar Ahmed Siddiqui became the Director (Vice Chancellor) of Sukkur Institute of Business Administration in 2004, after gaining wide experience in Management, Administration, and Academia at national and international levels.

Mr.Siddiqui

Mr. Siddiqui obtained his full time Masters in Economics from University of Sindh, Jamshoro. Initially he worked as a school teacher teaching Mathematics, Economics and English language for ten years.

Mr.Siddiqui

He then obtained another Masters degree in Education from University of Sindh Jamshoro. His academic achievement was rewarded with a Gold Medal.
